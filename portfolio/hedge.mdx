# Hedge

The `Hedge` tab highlights how strategies offset or reinforce each other during shared market periods.

> [GIF placeholder: Reviewing hedge pair classifications]

---

## Why this matters

- Reduces accidental overexposure.
- Reveals whether diversification is real or superficial.
- Helps size complementary strategies more confidently.

---

## How to use this tab

1. Enable your target strategy mix.
2. Inspect hedge pairings and overlap details.
3. Reduce combinations that amplify the same directional risk.
4. Re-check [Correlation](/portfolio/correlation) and [Analytics](/portfolio/analytics) after edits.

> [Image placeholder: Hedge tracker with overlap details]

---

## Best practices

- Treat heavy same-session overlap as a risk signal.
- Use hedge insights before running `Optimize`.
- Validate improvements with `Simulations` before deploying.
