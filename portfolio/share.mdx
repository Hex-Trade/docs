# Share

Use `Share` to distribute a portfolio configuration so collaborators can review or import the same setup.

> [GIF placeholder: Sharing a portfolio configuration]

---

## Why share portfolios

- Standardize review workflows.
- Reduce copy/paste mistakes in manual setup.
- Preserve a known-good configuration for later use.

---

## Share workflow

1. Build and validate your portfolio.
2. Click `Share` from the builder header.
3. Create the share output and send it to your reviewer.
4. Recipient imports and validates before live use.

> [Image placeholder: Share portfolio modal]

---

## Security and operations

- Share only intended strategy data.
- Avoid posting private identifiers in public channels.
- Re-validate all settings after importing a shared portfolio.
