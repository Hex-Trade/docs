# Portfolio Calendar

The `Calendar` tab in Portfolio helps you identify **when** gains and losses happen across your strategy mix.

> [GIF placeholder: Navigating months in Portfolio calendar]

---

## What this view shows

- Daily P&L outcomes.
- Monthly return distribution.
- Clusters of strong or weak periods.
- Changes after enabling news filters or different allocations.

---

## How to use it

1. Open `Portfolio` and switch to the `Calendar` tab.
2. Review winning and losing day concentration.
3. Compare month-to-month consistency.
4. Cross-check with [Portfolio Trades](/portfolio/trades) to inspect specific sessions.

> [Image placeholder: Portfolio calendar heatmap]

---

## Common use cases

- Validate whether performance depends on a narrow date window.
- Spot fragile behavior before live deployment.
- Compare two portfolio versions using chart snapshots and calendar distribution.

<Note>
This page is for the **Portfolio tab** calendar, not the macro event feed. For economic events, use [Economic Calendar](/calendar).
</Note>
