## How to connect

1. Go to [dash.hextrade.io/algos](https://dash.hextrade.io/algos) and search for the Raptor variant you want.
2. Open the detail page and review the metrics: APY, win rate, total trades, and P&L.
3. Check that the variant supports your connected broker.
4. Go to **Connected Accounts**, select your account, and set your risk level.
5. Activate — Hextrade will execute trades automatically based on the algorithm's signals.

> [Image placeholder: Raptor detail page and connected accounts panel]

---

<Warning>
  Each Raptor variant is tuned for a different instrument. Do not connect an account funded in an incompatible currency or asset class. Always start with micro contract sizes to verify the integration before scaling.
</Warning>
