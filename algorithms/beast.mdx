# Beast

Beast is a 5-minute strategy running on Nasdaq 100 futures (NQ).

---

## Available Variants

| Channel | Instrument | Timeframe |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| `nq-beast-5m` | Nasdaq 100 (NQ) | 5 minutes |

---

## Connecting Beast

1. Go to [dash.hextrade.io/algos](https://dash.hextrade.io/algos) and find `nq-beast-5m`.
2. Review performance metrics on the detail page.
3. Connect your broker account, set risk, and activate.

<Warning>
  5-minute strategies generate frequent signals. Monitor your account closely when first activating and ensure your broker supports the trade volume.
</Warning>
