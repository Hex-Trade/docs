# POW

POW is a fast intraday strategy focused on Nasdaq futures (`NQ`) for traders who want a high-reactivity profile.

## How to connect

1. Go to [dash.hextrade.io/algos](https://dash.hextrade.io/algos) and find `nq-pow`.
2. Review performance metrics on the detail page.
3. Connect your broker account, set risk, and activate.

> [Image placeholder: POW connected account setup]

<Warning>
  Intraday strategies generate frequent signals. Monitor your account closely when first activating and ensure your broker supports the trade volume.
</Warning>
