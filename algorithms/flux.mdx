# Flux

Flux is a very short-timeframe algorithm (4 minutes) running on Gold futures (GC). It is one of the highest-frequency strategies in the Hextrade library.

---

## Available Variants

| Channel | Instrument | Timeframe |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| `gc-flux-4m` | Gold (GC) | 4 minutes |

---

## Connecting Flux

1. Go to [dash.hextrade.io/algos](https://dash.hextrade.io/algos) and find `gc-flux-4m`.
2. Review performance metrics and trade frequency on the detail page.
3. Connect your broker account and set risk carefully.
4. Activate.

<Warning>
  4-minute algorithms are among the highest-frequency strategies available. High trade volume means higher cumulative commission costs. Only use Flux if your broker's fee structure supports high-frequency activity.
</Warning>
