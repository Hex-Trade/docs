# Volt

Volt is a short-timeframe strategy running on a 17-minute chart across Nasdaq 100 (NQ) and S&P 500 (ES).

---

## Available Variants

| Channel | Instrument | Timeframe |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| `nq-volt-17m` | Nasdaq 100 (NQ) | 17 minutes |
| `es-volt-17m` | S&P 500 (ES) | 17 minutes |

---

## Connecting Volt

1. Go to [dash.hextrade.io/algos](https://dash.hextrade.io/algos) and find the Volt variant.
2. Review metrics on the detail page.
3. Connect your broker account and set risk.
4. Activate.

<Warning>
  Short-timeframe algorithms trade more frequently. Monitor your account closely when first activating and ensure your broker's commission structure is compatible with higher trade volume.
</Warning>
