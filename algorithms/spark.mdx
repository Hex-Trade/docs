# ⚡ Spark

Spark is a 30-minute strategy available on Nasdaq 100 (NQ) and Gold (GC).

---

## Available Variants

| Channel | Instrument | Timeframe |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| `nq-spark-30m` | Nasdaq 100 (NQ) | 30 minutes |
| `gc-spark-30m` | Gold (GC) | 30 minutes |

---

## Connecting Spark

1. Go to [dash.hextrade.io/algos](https://dash.hextrade.io/algos) and find the Spark variant.
2. Review performance metrics on the detail page.
3. Connect your broker account via **Connected Accounts**.
4. Set risk level and activate.

<Warning>
  Trading involves risk of loss. Always verify performance metrics and use appropriate position sizing before going live.
</Warning>
