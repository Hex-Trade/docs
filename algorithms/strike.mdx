# Strike

Strike is a 5-minute algorithm running on Gold futures (GC).

---

## Available Variants

| Channel | Instrument | Timeframe |
|---------|-----------|----------|
| `gc-strike-5m` | Gold (GC) | 5 minutes |

---

## Connecting Strike

1. Go to [dash.hextrade.io/algos](https://dash.hextrade.io/algos) and find `gc-strike-5m`.
2. Review performance metrics before connecting.
3. Link your broker account via **Connected Accounts**.
4. Set risk and activate.

<Warning>
  5-minute strategies generate frequent signals and can accumulate commissions quickly. Ensure your account has sufficient margin and review commission costs with your broker.
</Warning>
