# Discord Community

Join our Discord server to get help and connect with other traders.

**[Join Discord](https://discord.com/invite/hextrade)**

## What you’ll find

- **Resource sharing** — Educational materials and tips to improve your trading.
- **Q&A and support** — Ask questions and get help from the community and team.
- **Trading channels** — Share and learn from others using algorithms or manual trading.
- **Algorithm updates** — Stay updated on algorithms and how they work.

## Disclaimer

Our Discord is for learning and community only. It is **not** certified financial advice. Treat all discussions as educational and do your own research before making trading decisions.
