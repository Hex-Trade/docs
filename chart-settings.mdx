# Chart Settings

**Chart settings** let you customize how your trading charts look and behave.

## Opening chart settings

- Open **Chart settings** from the sidebar, or
- Use the chart settings / gear icon from the **Trade** page.

## What you can change

### Appearance

- **Theme** — e.g. Dark, Light, TradingView-style, or Professional.
- **Background color** — Use the color picker for a custom background.
- **Chart style** — Candlestick, Line, Area, or Bars.

### Colors

- **Bull / bear** — Up and down candle (or bar) colors.
- **Wicks** — Candle wick color.
- **Grid** — Grid line color and opacity.
- **Labels** — Text and axis label color.

### Chart elements

- **Grid** — Show or hide the grid.
- **Volume** — Show or hide volume bars.
- **Crosshair** — Turn the crosshair on or off.
- **Legend** — Show or hide the legend.
- **Line width** — Thickness of lines (e.g. 1–5 px).

### Text

- **Font size** — Typically 8–20 px.
- **Font family** — e.g. Inter, Arial, Monospace.
- **Label opacity** — How transparent the labels are.

## Saving

Your choices are saved automatically (e.g. in your browser). They apply when you open the trading chart again. Changes apply to the TradingView-style chart used on the platform.
