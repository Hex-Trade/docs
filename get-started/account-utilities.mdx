# ️ Account Utilities

Once logged in, Hextrade gives you full control over your profile, appearance, and account settings. Here's everything you can do.

---

##  Light Mode vs. Dark Mode

Hextrade offers both **Light** and **Dark** modes to suit your visual preferences and reduce eye strain during long trading sessions.

<Steps>
<Step title="Locate the Settings Icon">
On the dashboard, find the settings or theme icon in the **top-right corner** of the screen.
</Step>
<Step title="Access Theme Options">
Click the icon to open theme options. You'll see a toggle labeled **Light Mode / Dark Mode**.
</Step>
<Step title="Switch the Theme">
Flip the toggle to select your desired mode. Changes apply **instantly**.
</Step>
<Step title="Preferences are saved">
Your theme preference is saved automatically and persists across sessions.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
The **Hextrade Aqua** theme (dark background with cyan accents) is the native brand theme — recommended for the best visual experience.
</Tip>

---

## ️ Fullscreen Mode

Maximize your workspace for an immersive trading experience.

<Steps>
<Step title="Locate the Fullscreen Icon">
Find the fullscreen icon in the **top-right menu** of the dashboard.
</Step>
<Step title="Enable Fullscreen">
Click the icon to expand the dashboard to fullscreen — ideal for monitoring charts and multiple accounts.
</Step>
<Step title="Exit Fullscreen">
Press the same icon again or hit **Escape** on your keyboard to return to normal view.
</Step>
</Steps>

---

##  Update Profile Details

Keep your profile information accurate and up to date.

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Profile Settings">
Navigate to the **Profile Settings** tab on the dashboard (accessible from your avatar or the settings menu).
</Step>
<Step title="Edit Your Details">
Update the following fields as needed:
- **First Name** and **Last Name**
- **Language** preference
- **Currency** display preference
</Step>
<Step title="Save Changes">
Click **Save** to apply your updates. Changes take effect immediately.
</Step>
</Steps>

---

##  Change Password

Secure your account by updating your password regularly.

<Steps>
<Step title="Go to Change Password">
In Profile Settings, switch to the **"Change Password"** tab.
</Step>
<Step title="Enter Current Password">
For verification, provide your existing password.
</Step>
<Step title="Set a New Password">
Input and confirm your new password. Make it strong and unique — at least 8 characters with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.
</Step>
<Step title="Save Changes">
Click **Save** to finalize the update. Your new password is active immediately.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
After changing your password, any other active sessions may be signed out. You'll need to log back in on those devices.
</Warning>

---

## ️ Change Profile Picture

Personalize your Hextrade profile with a custom avatar.

<Steps>
<Step title="Click the Profile Icon">
Click on the profile icon next to your name in the settings menu.
</Step>
<Step title="Upload a Picture">
Select an image from your device. Supported formats: **JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP**. Max size: **5 MB**.
</Step>
<Step title="Save Changes">
Click **Save** to upload and apply your new profile picture. It will appear in the header across the platform.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Your profile picture is stored securely and is visible on your profile and the leaderboard.
</Tip>
