## ğŸ”‘ Forgot Your Password?

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click Forgot Password">
    On the login page, click the **"Forgot Password"** link below the sign-in button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your email">
    Enter the email address linked to your account and submit.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check your inbox">
    You'll receive a password reset link. Click it to set a new password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a new password">
    Choose a strong new password, confirm it, and save. You can now log in with your new credentials.
  </Step>
</Steps>
