# Billing

The `Billing` section provides visibility into your active plan, renewal timing, and billing actions.

> [GIF placeholder: Opening billing portal from settings]

---

## What you can manage

- Current subscription status.
- Next billing date and remaining period.
- Plan cost and billing cadence.
- Promo code application.
- Billing portal access.

---

## Standard workflow

1. Review current status and renewal date.
2. Open `Manage billing` for payment method or subscription updates.
3. Apply promo codes when eligible.
4. Re-check plan-limited features in [Subscriptions](/subscriptions).

> [Image placeholder: Billing card with next billing date and status]

---

## Notes

- Plan level affects deployment and premium feature access.
- Billing changes can take a short time to reflect across all UI areas.
