# Notifications

Use `Notifications` to control where alerts are delivered and which events should trigger them.

> [GIF placeholder: Toggling channels and alert types]

---

## Delivery channels

- Discord webhook
- Discord direct messages
- Email alerts

You can also use the global master switch to quickly silence all alerts.

---

## Alert types

Configure triggers such as:

- Trade opened
- Trade closed
- Trade error
- Connection error
- Account updated
- System alert

---

## Sound alerts

- Toggle browser sound notifications for executions.
- Adjust volume to match your workflow environment.

> [Image placeholder: Notification settings panel with channel toggles]

---

## Best practices

- Keep connection and trade-error alerts enabled.
- Use Discord webhook for team visibility.
- Test settings after changes by saving and triggering a known event.
