# Appearance

The `Appearance` section lets you adjust visual style without changing trading behavior.

> [GIF placeholder: Switching dashboard themes]

---

## Theme selection

Choose from multiple themes and accent styles to match your preferred visual environment.

The active theme applies throughout the dashboard interface.

---

## Motion and effects

- Toggle animations globally.
- Disable motion effects to reduce visual noise or improve performance on slower devices.

> [Image placeholder: Theme grid and motion toggle]

---

## Recommendations

- Use a high-contrast theme for long trading sessions.
- Disable motion effects if you prioritize minimal distraction.
