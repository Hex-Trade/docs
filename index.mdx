# Welcome To Hextrade

Welcome to **Hextrade** — your gateway to smarter, more efficient trading! We're thrilled to have you on our AI copy-trade platform.

Hextrade is an innovative **copy-trade platform** that empowers traders by blending automation with AI. Use our advanced algorithms, set up copy trading across multiple accounts, or connect your own strategy via a **TradingView webhook**.

## Getting Started

<Steps>
<Step title="Create your account">
Sign up for free (while in Beta). Use email or Google.
[**Register at dash.hextrade.io →**](https://dash.hextrade.io/en/auth/register)
</Step>
<Step title="Connect a broker account">
Link your trading account from [Accounts](https://dash.hextrade.io/accounts). We support **ProjectX, Tradovate, NinjaTrader, Tradier, Oanda, DxTrade, Alpaca, CQG**, and more.
</Step>
<Step title="Choose your strategy">
Browse [Algorithms](https://dash.hextrade.io/algos) to run automated trades, set up [Copy Trading](/copy-trading/setup) across accounts, or connect your own strategy via [TradingView Webhook](/external-algorithms/tradingview-webhooks).
</Step>
<Step title="Monitor & optimize">
Use [Analytics](https://dash.hextrade.io/analytics) and the [Economic Calendar](https://dash.hextrade.io/calendar) to track performance and time your trades.
</Step>
</Steps>
