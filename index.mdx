# Welcome to Hextrade

Welcome to **Hextrade**, your gateway to smarter, more efficient trading. This guide explains how to use the platform.

## What is Hextrade?

Hextrade is an **AI copy-trade platform** that blends automation with AI. You can use our algorithms or connect your own via a TradingView webhook.

## What you can do

- **Manage accounts** — See all your connected trading accounts, portfolio value, and completed trades in one place.
- **Use algorithms** — Browse and use trading algorithms; view performance and details.
- **Copy trade** — Create groups with a master account and followers so trades are copied automatically.
- **Analyze performance** — Use analytics and the economic calendar to support your decisions.
- **Trade** — Use the full-screen trading view with charts and place orders.
- **Manage subscriptions** — View and manage your algorithm subscriptions.

## Get started

1. **[Create an account](https://dash.hextrade.io/en/auth/register)** — Sign up for free (while in Beta).
2. **Log in** — Go to the [dashboard](https://dash.hextrade.io) and sign in.
3. **Explore** — After login you’ll land on **Accounts**. Use the sidebar to open **Algos**, **Copy Trade**, **Analytics**, and more.

For step-by-step setup and first steps, see [Getting started](/getting-started). For help and community, join [Discord](/discord).
